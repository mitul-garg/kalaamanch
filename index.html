SKETCHATHON

An artist grows by leaps and bounds when he or she puts artwork in the public eye for feedback, and that is what our Sketchathons are all about. Each participant's artwork competes with other artworks to emerge as the Winner.

×

SKETCH BATTLE

A flower does not think of competing to the flower next to it.It just blooms. Similarly, artworks cannot be compared, they just flourish in their own way. Come and see them flourish in the Battles. The Winners are decided by one-on-one battles in form of a Knockout Tournament!
